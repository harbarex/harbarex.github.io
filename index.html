01 — About

Perception that turns pixels into understanding.

I'm a Senior Perception Engineer at Sauron, where I'm building an autonomous home security system powered by the perception stack of an autonomous vehicle — multi-sensor fusion, tracking, and real-time scene understanding running at the edge.

My work lives at the juncture of deep learning and computer vision. I'm drawn to challenging, meaningful real-world problems — scene understanding, tracking, and re-identification — and I love shipping systems that make those ideas reliable in production.

Previously I earned my M.S.E in CIS from the University of Pennsylvania and a B.Tech in CS from Punjab Engineering College. Along the way I was fortunate to be mentored by Prof. Jianbo Shi (GRASP Lab, Penn) and Prof. Jürgen Beyerer & Dr. Tim Zander (Fraunhofer IOSB / KIT, Germany).
