Unsupervised Deep Domain Adaptation for Object Detection

Studying domain shift and negative transfer in unsupervised object detection.

Aug 2019 Deep Learning
PDF

Research with Prof. Eric Granger on the limits of unsupervised domain adaptation for detection.

  • Analysed negative transfer (≈20% mAP drop from baseline) and catastrophic forgetting in image-to-image domain-adaptation approaches on face-detection datasets.
  • Studied local features and temporal information from trackers to generalise unsupervised domain adaptation on datasets like SCUT and WIDER FACE.
